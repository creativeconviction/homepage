About

Creative Conviction is a platform where we seek to spark conversation around capital, creativity, and AI.

We are Libbie and Rayouf, two investors at Bessemer Venture Partners who spend our days at the intersection of creativity, technology, and company building. We started Creative Conviction because we believe that taste and creative ambition are becoming the most important differentiators in the age of AI, and we wanted a place to explore what that means with the people living it.

Our methods include interviews with founders and creative leaders, curated gatherings, and written reports. If you would like to suggest a guest, propose a gathering, or send us a note, please reach out.
